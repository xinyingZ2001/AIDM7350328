Will Love in Spring Synopsis

"Will Love in Spring" is an urban romance drama produced by China Central Television, Tencent Video, Beijing Winter Sunshine Culture Media Co., Ltd., and Tianjin Motie Entertainment Co., Ltd. The series is directed by Cheng Liang and written by Jian Yiwen, starring Li Xian and Zhou Yutong, with a special appearance by Liu Lin. Fang Fang, Wu Junting, and Zhang Xiiwei have special appearances, and Wan Peng makes a guest appearance.


The drama is adapted from the novel "Lovers" by Douban Reading author Shemes. It tells the story of Chen Maidong, who becomes a mortuary cosmetologist after experiencing life changes. His high school classmate, Zhuang Jie, becomes disabled in a car accident and fights alone in a big city to become a career elite. Through their ongoing interactions, they gradually come to understand and respect each other, healing one another with love in Nanping Town.

Will Love in Spring Cast

Chen Maidong （Li Xian）

Chen Maidong, once a wealthy young man, is proud and sensitive. Due to family upheaval, his life takes a different path, leading him to become a mortuary cosmetologist. Although he appears indifferent, he harbors a deep love and persistence for life. Upon reuniting with Zhuang Jie, the two gradually draw closer through their interactions. As Chen Maidong witnesses both Zhuang Jie's vulnerability and strength, his inner defenses slowly crumble. He protects Zhuang Jie in his own way and, through her, rediscovers the meaning and hope in life.

Zhuang Jie （Zhou Yutong）

Zhuang Jie is an independent and resilient individual, serving as a successful creative director at an advertising company. She lost her right leg in a car accident, which led to a downturn in her life, yet she never bowed to fate. Through her interactions with Chen Maidong, she is gradually moved by his kindness and warmth, and together they grow by supporting each other. Zhuang Jie inspires those around her with her strength and optimism, and in choosing between love and career, she finds her own path to happiness.

Where is Nanping Town from Will Love in Spring?
Introduction to the Filming Locations

Quanzhou Chongwu Ancient City

When Zhuang Jie was delivering food on her electric bike, she passed by Chongwu Ancient City and met Chen Maidong who was watching Gaojia Opera at the seaside stage. Chongwu Ancient City has a history of more than 600 years and combines seaside scenery with historical charm.

Quanzhou Licheng District

Zhuang Jie's home in the play is located in the Old Posthouse on West Street, a Nanyang-style building built by overseas Chinese in the 1960s. In addition, the play also features many iconic attractions such as Kaiyuan Temple, East and West Towers, and Bell Tower.

Quanzhou Jiudu Town

Many scenes of Nanping Town in the drama were filmed here, such as the paragliding base where Chen Maidong runs in the morning, and the country road where Zhuang Jie and Chen Maidong stroll. The quiet rural atmosphere adds a lot of simplicity and warmth to the drama.
